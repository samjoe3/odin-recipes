Chicken Alfredo

Creamy Homemade Chicken Alfredo From Scratch

This delicious chicken alfredo is easy to make, and doesn't compromise on flavour.
After making this two-ingredient-sauce you will never go back to store-bought alfredo sauce again.

Ingredients

  • One Cup Parmesan
  • One Cup Heavy Cream
  • One Box Linguine Noodles
  • One Pound Chicken ~Cubed

Directions

  1. Boil Noodles
  2. Pan Sear Chicken
  3. Heat Heavy Cream
  4. Stir-in Parmesan
  5. Mix Ingredients
